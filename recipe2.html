Dal Makhani

Preparation Time: 12 Hours (Includes soaking)

Difficulty Level: Hard

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Whole Black Lentils (Urad Dal)
  • 1/4 cup Kidney Beans (Rajma)
  • 3 tbsp Butter (Unsalted)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Cream
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Salt, Red Chili Powder, Kasoori Methi

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Soak the black lentils and kidney beans overnight (at least 8-10 hours).
  2. Pressure cook the soaked dal with water and salt until soft and mushy.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat butter and sauté ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add tomato puree and chili powder; cook until the butter separates.
  5. Add the boiled dal and mash some of it with the back of the spoon for a creamy texture.
  6. Simmer on very low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Stir in fresh cream and crushed Kasoori methi before serving.
