Aloo Paratha

Preparation Time: 30 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Whole wheat flour (Atta)
  • 3 medium Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 Green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Carom seeds (Ajwain)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Ghee or Butter for roasting

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare Dough: Knead the wheat flour with water and a pinch of salt into a soft dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Prepare Stuffing: In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, green chilies, ajwain, garam masala, salt, and coriander.
  3. Take a small ball of dough, flatten it, and place a spoonful of the potato mixture in the center.
  4. Bring the edges together to seal the stuffing and roll it out gently into a circle.
  5. Place the paratha on a hot tawa (griddle) and cook both sides until golden brown, applying ghee or butter.
  6. Serve hot with a dollop of white butter, curd, or pickle.
