Chole Bhature

Preparation Time: 60 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Hard

Ingredients

  • For Chole: 2 cups Chickpeas (soaked), 2 Onions (finely chopped), 2 Tomatoes (pureed), 1 tbsp Chole Masala, 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste.
  • For Bhature: 2 cups All-purpose flour (Maida), 1/2 cup Yogurt, 1/2 tsp Baking soda, Water for kneading, Oil for deep frying.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare Chole: Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with tea bags (for color) and salt until tender.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste in oil until golden brown. Add tomato puree and spices.
  3. Mix in the cooked chickpeas and simmer until the gravy thickens. Garnish with coriander.
  4. Prepare Bhature: Mix flour, baking soda, and yogurt. Knead into a smooth dough and rest for 2 hours.
  5. Roll into ovals and deep fry in hot oil until they puff up and turn golden.
  6. Serve hot with pickles and sliced onions.
