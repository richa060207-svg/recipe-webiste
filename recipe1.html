Paneer Butter Masala

Preparation Time: 40 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (Cottage Cheese) cubes
  • 3 large Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1 tbsp Dried Fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a pan and sauté the ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
  2. Add the tomato puree and cook on medium heat until the oil begins to separate from the gravy.
  3. Stir in the garam masala and salt to taste.
  4. Add the paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Finish by stirring in the heavy cream and crushed Kasuri methi for a rich aroma.
  6. Serve hot with Naan or Roti.
