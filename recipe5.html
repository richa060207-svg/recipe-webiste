Gulab Jamun

Preparation Time: 45 Minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk Powder
  • 1/4 cup All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • 1 tsp Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 2-3 tbsp Milk (for kneading)
  • 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  • For Syrup: 1.5 cups Sugar, 1 cup Water, 3 Cardamom pods, Saffron strands.
  • Oil or Ghee for deep frying

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare Syrup: Boil water and sugar with cardamom and saffron until it reaches a sticky consistency. Keep it warm.
  2. Make Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk slowly to form a soft, smooth dough. Do not over-knead.
  3. Divide the dough into small, crack-free balls.
  4. Fry: Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn a deep golden brown.
  5. Soak: Immediately drop the fried balls into the warm sugar syrup.
  6. Let them soak for at least 2 hours until they double in size and become spongy.
